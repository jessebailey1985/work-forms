Outbound Shipment Planning

Lanes Update Exceptions Performance

Lane A: Core Operations

Load Planning Execution

Goal: Execute routing and billing on time and within all service level agreements (SLAs).

Core Functions:

  • Routing Ensure routes are planned according to established business requirements and parameters.
  • Billing: Ensure accurate transition of completed orders between systems (WMS, TMS, etc.).
  • Standard Communication: Handling general email correspondence related to planned routes.

Key Metrics:

  • Cube per Mile (Efficiency)
  • Cube per Departure (Utilization)
  • On-Time Billing to Warehouses (Compliance)

Lane B: Exception-Based Change Management

Immediate Resolution and Data Integrity

Goal: Maintain stability of the workflow by engaging exceptions, resolving immediate issues, and ensuring system data reflects reality.

Core Functions:

  • AD-HOC Management: End-to-End coverage for urgent, unplanned AD-HOCs and Add-Ons (phone/email).
  • Proactive Communication: Responds to critical inquiries and engages appropriate teams as needed for real-time issues.
  • Parameters Upkeep: Execution, maintenance, and data-entry for all shipment planning rules, curfews, NLTs, and ODS.
  • Transfers: Coordinates the completion of non-standard, urgent transfers (STS/STW).
  • Delivery Changes: Ensures notifications are sent out immediately for system/delivery changes, and tracking is updated.

Key Metrics:

  • Successful Execution of Ad-HOCs (Resolution Rate)
  • Reduction of Rework and Out-of-Scope Tasks
  • Data Integrity Score (Audited by Lane C)

Lane C: Compliance & Performance Analysis

Quality & Optimization

Goal: Maintain the feedback loop for continuous improvement and ensure system parameters reflect best practice.

Core Functions:

  • NLT Compliance Analysis: Auditing routes for adherence to delivery windows and reporting on failure root cause.
  • Overload Analysis: Reviewing violation reports for systemic or training-based root causes.
  • VTAs (Individually Assigned ): Develops and documents process steps, standards, and best practices.
  • System Audit: Auditing the Parameter data managed by Lane B to ensure accuracy and compliance.
  • AD-HOC Root Cause Analysis: Analyzing patterns in exceptions from Lane B to recommend systemic fixes (to parameters/training).

Key Metrics:

  • On-Time Rate (Compliant Routes/Total Routes)
  • Overloads per 1,000 Routes (Reduction)
  • Roll out of Load Planning Playbook (Documentation Status)
  • Reduction in AD-HOC Frequency (Effectiveness of fixes)
